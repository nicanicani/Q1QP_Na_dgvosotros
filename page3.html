Conclusion

Our report contains the number of six (6) different columns that indicate the Album Title, Artist, Group Category, Type of Album, Retail Album Sales, and the Album sales in the kpop industry. An interesting fact about our data set is that It is shown in our data that there are 21 girl groups that had the best retail albums and albums alone back in 2022. That is indeed the majority out of the 4 different group categories combined. Although, there are some problems that could happen in the future. For example, our database was based on opinions and theories with no official claims at all. For another instance, it could be that the website we got our information from may come out not being accurate. To address these problems, we believe that finding a trustable and updated source would solve these two.
