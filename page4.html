Our data set comes from Circle Chart or previously known as Gaon Music Chart. This website tabulates the relative weekly popularity of songs or albums in South Korea. It is compiled by the Korea Music Content Association and sponsored by South Korea's Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism.
